My Project Timeline

A journey through my work
Jan 2023 - Present

Molecular Dynamics based analysis of Protein Sequencing through Nanopore

Studying the interactions of protein analyte passing through the CsgG nanopore, usually present in Oxford Nanopore Technology (ONT) devices. This research aims to enhance sequencing accuracy and efficiency by identifying residues of CsgG which could be altered.

Oct 2022 - Dec 2022

Variational Autoencoder for Protein Design

Developed a Variational Autoencoder model that created a 3D latent space for a protein family, then implemented a logistic regression classifier to distinguish different GO-annotated proteins within this family. Successfully generated novel protein sequences validated by embedding distances on a Poincare ball hyperboloid.

Jul 2022 - Sep 2022

Acetylcholine Unbinding Simulation

Simulated the unbinding process of Acetylcholine from the M2R-Go protein complex using specialized molecular dynamics techniques. Compared full structure behavior versus just the transmembrane region to understand G protein effects, revealing important residues, loop interactions, and binding pocket changes.

Jan 2022 - Jun 2022

Biosurfactant Production in E. coli

Engineered E. coli to produce Alasan and Rhamnolipid biosurfactants using a bi-directional promoter system. Performed MD simulations to analyze micelle formation by Rhamnolipid in the presence of copper ions and decane, providing insights into their molecular mechanisms.
